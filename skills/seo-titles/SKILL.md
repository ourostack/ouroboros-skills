# SEO Title Tag Skill

## Core Principle
A title tag is an ad in search results. It competes for clicks against 9 other results. A keyword-stuffed title that nobody clicks is worse than a compelling title with one keyword.

## Rules

1. **50-60 characters.** Google truncates at ~60. Every character after that is invisible. Count before committing.

2. **Primary keyword in the first 5 words.** Search engines weight early terms higher. "What Is Agent Experience" beats "Understanding the Concept of Agent Experience."

3. **One keyword, naturally.** Never repeat the same word. "Agent Harness for AI Agents" has "agent" twice — that's stuffing, not SEO.

4. **Trigger curiosity or address a pain.** People click on titles that make them feel something:
   - Curiosity: "What If Your Agent Never Forgot Who It Was?"
   - Pain: "Why Your AI Agent Forgets Everything Between Sessions"
   - Promise: "Build an AI Agent in 150 Lines of TypeScript"
   - Challenge: "Persistence Isn't Enough for AI Agents"

5. **Match search intent.** Ask: "What would someone type into Google to find this page?" The title should feel like the answer to that query.

6. **Brand at the end.** `| Ouroboros` or `— Ouroboros` at the end, not the beginning. Exception: homepage can lead with the brand.

7. **No filler words.** "A Comprehensive Guide to Understanding" → "How to." Every word must earn its place.

8. **Specific > vague.**
   - Bad: "Getting Started"
   - Good: "Get Started in 3 Minutes"
   - Bad: "Documentation"
   - Good: "Docs — Psyche, Memory, Architecture"

9. **Questions work.** "What Is an Agent Harness?" directly matches the search query. Questions also imply the page has the answer.

10. **Test: would you click this?** Read the title as one of 10 blue links on a Google results page. If it doesn't stand out, rewrite it.

## Anti-patterns
- Keyword stuffing: "AI Agent Harness for Persistent AI Agents"
- Too vague: "Why Ouroboros"
- Too long: anything over 60 chars
- Brand first (non-homepage): "Ouroboros | Getting Started Guide"
- Filler: "A Complete Guide to Understanding How..."
- All caps power words: "ULTIMATE Guide" "BEST Framework"

## Process
1. Identify the primary search query this page answers
2. Write the title as a response to that query with curiosity/pain/promise
3. Count characters (must be ≤60)
4. Check: primary keyword in first 5 words?
5. Check: no repeated words?
6. Check: would you click this over 9 competitors?
7. Add brand suffix if not homepage
